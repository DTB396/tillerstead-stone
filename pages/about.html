Tillerstead LLC is a licensed New Jersey home improvement contractor specializing in tile installation, waterproof shower assemblies, and interior remodeling.

Background

Tyler is a licensed contractor with experience in tile installation, waterproofing, and interior remodeling. Work includes occupied homes, rental properties, and coordination with general contractors on larger scopes.

Projects follow TCNA tile installation standards and ANSI waterproofing specifications. Scopes are documented with written proposals, progress photos, and closeout checklists.

Process

  • Written scope: Materials, timeline, payment terms, and change-order process documented before work begins.
  • Photo logs: Substrate prep, waterproofing details, and tile layout documented during install.
  • Communication: Regular updates on progress, schedule changes, or field conditions that affect scope.
  • Closeout: Final inspection checklist, care instructions, and warranty terms provided at project completion.

Licensing & location
