Project Planning & DIY Essentials

Start here if you're planning a project, gathering estimates, or want to understand the scope before committing. These tools help demystify the renovation process and keep your sanity intact.

Room By Room Project Planner: Organize Interior Design Ideas & Contractor Quotes

Planning & Organization Tool

Track room layouts, design ideas, contractor quotes, material purchases, and timelines in one place. Especially useful when coordinating multiple trades or phasing a larger renovation. Keeps all the details you will forget if you don't write them down.

Pro tip: Use this to document existing conditions before demo. Take photos, sketch measurements, and note electrical/plumbing locations. Your future self (and your contractor) will thank you.

BOSCH GLM100-23 Laser Distance Measure (100 Ft Range)

Measurement Tool | ±1/16" Accuracy

Makes estimate calls faster and more accurate. Measure rooms, calculate square footage, and verify dimensions solo without the tape measure dance. We use this on every initial walk-through—saves time and eliminates the "I thought the room was bigger" conversation later.

Installation tip: Measure corner to corner in multiple spots to verify rooms are square. Out-of-square rooms need layout adjustments that affect material calculations.

DGSL Tile Leveling System (1200 Spacers + 300 Wedges + Plier)

DIY Installation Essential | 1/16" Joint Width

The DIY secret weapon for lippage-free installations. Keeps tiles level during thinset cure, preventing the "one tile higher than the next" disaster. Works on floors and walls. This system eliminates 90% of common DIY tile mistakes.

Sizing guidance: This kit includes 1/16" spacers. Not sure which joint size you need? It depends on tile size, rectified vs non-rectified edges, and your substrate flatness. Contact us with your tile specs and we'll recommend the right size—takes 2 minutes and prevents ordering the wrong kit.

Cotton Farm Grade 100 XL Cheesecloth (36x36", Unbleached)

Grout Cleanup Essential | Ultra-Dense Butter Muslin

The pro's choice for haze-free grout cleanup. Standard sponges push grout around—cheesecloth pulls haze without disturbing joints. Reusable, washable, and way more effective than the disposable alternatives. One pack handles 2-3 average bathrooms.

Technique: Dampen lightly, wring nearly dry, then buff in circular motions. Flip to clean side frequently. Let grout cure 72 hours before final buffing for best results.

Waterproofing Systems

The most critical layer. Read our full comparison: RedGard vs KERDI vs HYDRO BAN

Custom Building Products RedGard Waterproofing Membrane (1 Gallon)

ANSI A118.10 Compliant | Liquid-applied membrane

The budget-friendly workhorse for cement board prep. Apply 2+ coats with mesh reinforcement at plane changes. We use this on 90% of standard tub surrounds and shower remodels where the substrate is already flat and stable.

Installation tip: Use a wet-film gauge to verify 30-35 mil thickness per coat. Don't skip the 24-hour cure before flood testing.

Schluter KERDI 200 Waterproofing Membrane Roll (3'3" x 33')

ANSI A118.10 Compliant | Sheet membrane system

The gold standard for full shower rebuilds. Bonds directly to substrate with unmodified thinset. Integrates seamlessly with KERDI-Drain systems. Higher upfront cost, but testable seams and manufacturer warranty make it the right choice for high-value homes.

Installation tip: Must use unmodified thinset (ANSI A118.1). Modified mortars prevent proper bonding to the polyethylene core.

LATICRETE HYDRO BAN Board (32" x 48" x 1/2")

ANSI A118.10 Compliant | Tile backer + integrated waterproofing

All-in-one substrate that eliminates separate waterproofing steps. Ideal for retrofit showers where demo is limited. More expensive per square foot, but labor savings offset cost on smaller projects.

Installation tip: Still requires HYDRO BAN sealant at seams and penetrations. Not a "set and forget" system—follow the installation guide religiously.

Mortars & Thinsets

Large-format tile guidance: Flatness, Mortars, & Trowels

Mapei Ultraflex LFT Large Format Tile Mortar (50 lbs)

ANSI A118.15 / ISO 13007 | Modified large-format mortar

Non-sag performance for vertical installations up to 18" tiles. Extended open time for large-format work. What we use for rectified porcelain on walls and floors that demand consistent coverage.

Mixing ratio: 6-7 quarts of water per 50 lb bag. Mix to mayonnaise consistency—too wet causes slump.

Custom Building Products VersaBond Fortified Thinset (50 lbs)

ANSI A118.4 / A118.11 | Modified medium-bed mortar

The reliable all-around performer for ceramic and standard porcelain up to 12". Polymer-modified for bond strength. Available at every Home Depot, making it ideal for emergency repairs or small projects.

Schluter SET Unmodified Thinset Mortar (50 lbs)

ANSI A118.1 | Unmodified for KERDI systems

Required for bonding Schluter membranes. Unmodified formulation allows proper bond to polyethylene. Not interchangeable with modified mortars—check your membrane manufacturer's requirements.

Grouts

Mapei Ultracolor Plus FA Rapid-Set Grout (10 lbs)

ANSI A118.7 | Polymer-modified rapid-set

Consistent color, minimal efflorescence, fast cure. The grout we use on 80% of residential jobs. Dense formula resists staining better than traditional cement grouts. Ready for light traffic in 3 hours.

Pro tip: Mix small batches (2-3 lbs at a time). Pot life is only 30-45 minutes. Clean tools immediately—this stuff sets fast.

Laticrete SpectraLOCK PRO Premium Epoxy Grout Kit

ANSI A118.3 | Epoxy grout system

Chemical-resistant, stain-proof, and nearly bulletproof. Overkill for most residential installs, but mandatory for commercial kitchens, lab spaces, or anywhere grout needs to survive aggressive cleaning.

Installation warning: Harder to work with than cement grout. Requires experience and speed. Not DIY-friendly unless you've practiced on sample boards.

Tools & Equipment

QEP 24" Aluminum Tile Saw Fence

Accurate cuts for rectified large-format tile. Essential for maintaining 1/16" joint consistency. The difference between "it's fine" and "it looks professional."

Marshalltown 11" x 11" U-Notch Trowel (1/2" x 1/2")

Large-format tile coverage tool

The right trowel for 12"+ tiles. Smaller notches don't provide the coverage needed for proper bond. Follow TCNA method for back-buttering tiles over 15" to ensure 95%+ coverage.

Empire 48" True Blue Laser Level

For checking substrate flatness before tile installation. Validates TCNA flatness tolerance (1/8" in 10'). Worth the investment—prevents lippage disasters before they start.

Rubi DW-250N Wet Tile Saw (10")

The workhorse saw for porcelain and large-format cuts. Direct drive, minimal blade deflection. Expensive but pays for itself in clean cuts and time saved on complex layouts.

DIY alternative: Rent from Home Depot for $60/day if you're doing one project. Only buy if you're planning multiple installs.
